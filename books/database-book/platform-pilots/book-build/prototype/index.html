Book Purpose

Teach students how data becomes business performance

The prototype follows the manuscript's central logic: data becomes tables, tables become relationships, relationships make queries possible, and queries support analytics and decisions.

Target Audience

  • Undergraduate business students
  • Students new to databases and SQL
  • Managers and analysts who need system thinking
  • Instructors who want a reader plus lab workflow

Platform Principles

  • Responsive on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Accessible colors and readable typography
  • No backend in the prototype
  • No real login or payment processing
