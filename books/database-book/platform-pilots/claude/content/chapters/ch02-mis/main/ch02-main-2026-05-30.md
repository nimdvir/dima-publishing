## What Is an Information System?

An information system is the combination of people, processes, and technology that
turns data into decisions. The database is the storage layer, but the system around
it is what creates value.

## From Data to Decisions

As introduced in Chapter 1, data becomes information when placed in context. A
business adds two more steps: turning information into insight, and insight into
action.

```text
Data -> Information -> Insight -> Decision
```

## Business Value

Good information systems shorten the distance between a question and a confident
answer. That speed is the real competitive advantage. We will apply this directly
when we start writing queries in Chapter 5.
