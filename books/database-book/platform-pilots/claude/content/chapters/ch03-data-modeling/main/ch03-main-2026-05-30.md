---
title: Data Modeling
---

## Entities

An entity is something the business needs to track: a customer, an order, a
product. Each entity becomes a table.

## Attributes

Attributes are the facts we record about an entity. A customer has a name, an
email, and a signup date. Each attribute becomes a column.

## Relationships

Relationships connect entities. A customer places many orders. An order contains
many products. Modeling these links well is what keeps data clean and queries
simple later on.
