## 2. Legal Entity: Why a NY Business Corporation (Not an Education Corporation)

### Bottom Line

**Do NOT form an education corporation.** Form a regular **New York Business Corporation**.

### Why Not an Education Corporation?

- Your business is **publishing/media/content that serves education**, not an educational institution itself.
- NY Education Law §216: corporate purposes promoting "education in any way" may require **prior consent from the NY State Education Department (NYSED)**.
- Education corporations face: more oversight, slower formation, possible agency review, regulatory ambiguity.
- Being an "education corporation" does **not** improve tax deductions. Deductibility depends on whether expenses are *ordinary and necessary* for your trade or business — not on entity type.

### Deductible Business Expenses (regardless of entity type)

| Expense | Business Rationale |
|---------|-------------------|
| Domain registration | Publishing/company website |
| Web hosting | Sales, marketing, companion materials |
| Adobe/Canva/Figma/GitHub/AI tools | Production and courseware development |
| ISBNs | Publishing operations |
| Editing/proofreading | Book production |
| Cover/layout design | Product development |
| Legal/accounting | Formation and compliance |
| Marketing | Launch and sales |
| Database hosting | Companion labs and teaching materials |
| Contractor payments | Production/editorial support |

### Entity Type Decision Matrix

| Entity Type | Fit for Dima Publishing? |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Business Corporation** | ✅ **Best fit** — selling books, media, digital products, software, services, licenses |
| Benefit Corporation | ❌ Only if you want a legally stated public-benefit mission + extra reporting |
| Professional Service Corp | ❌ For licensed professions (law, medicine, accounting, etc.) |
| Not-for-Profit Corporation | ❌ Wrong if you want owner income, deductions, and commercial flexibility |
| LLC (Articles of Organization) | ❌ NY LLCs have a **publication requirement** you want to avoid |
| Professional Service LLC | ❌ For licensed professional services |
| Limited Partnership | ❌ Not appropriate for a solo publishing company |

### Certificate of Incorporation: Recommended Settings

| Field | Recommended Entry |
|-------|-------------------|
| Entity type | Business Corporation |
| Filing | Certificate of Incorporation |
| Name | **Dima Publishing, Inc.** |
| Purpose | Standard all-purpose clause |
| County | Albany County (unless you prefer NYC) |
| Stock | 200 shares, no par value |
| Filing fee | $125 |
| Incorporator | You |

### Language to AVOID in the Certificate

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| "to promote education" | May trigger NYSED consent |
| "to provide instruction" | Could imply regulated education services |
| "to operate an academy" | Sounds like a school |
| "to train students" | Could raise proprietary-school questions |
| "to grant certificates" | Could suggest credentialing |
| "to provide educational programs" | May invite agency review |
| "to advance knowledge" | Overlaps with Education Law §216 wording |

### Safe Purpose Language

| Use Instead |
|-------------|
| "publishing" |
| "media production" |
| "digital content development" |
| "software and database content" |
| "licensing of intellectual property" |
| "any lawful business activity" (broadest, safest) |

**Public descriptor:** *"Dima Publishing develops textbooks, digital learning materials, courseware, instructor resources, and data-driven educational media for business and information systems education."*

## 3. NY Department of State Online Filing — Step-by-Step

### Navigation Path

1. Go to the NY DOS Online Filing System
2. Select: **On-Line Filings for Business Owners**
3. Select: **Domestic Business Corporation (For Profit)**

### Do NOT Select

| Option | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| Domestic Limited Liability Company | LLC route — triggers publication requirement |
| On-Line Filings for 3rd Party Non-Service Company Filers | Only for filing on behalf of a client |
| Retrieve a Saved Transaction | Only if you already started a filing |
| Authenticate / Authenticate Document | Not for forming a new corporation |

### Key Filing Details

- **Legal name:** `Dima Publishing, Inc.`
- **Purpose clause:** Use the standard all-purpose clause — keep it broad and boring
- **Avoid any "education" language** in the legal filing
- Reference: <https://dos.ny.gov/certificate-incorporation-domestic-business-corporation>
